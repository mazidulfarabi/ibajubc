About Us

Background

Inception: 2009

Total Events: 50+

Total Members: 75 (Currently)

Mission, Vission and Motto

Mission:

Transcending leadership and cultivating well-rounded individuals for success in the dynamic corporate world through workshops, seminars, and collaborative events.

Vision:

To be the leading catalyst for holistic student development at IBA-JU, the Business Club strives to create a vibrant platform that transcends classroom knowledge. We aspire to continually collaborate with renowned organizations, preparing students to thrive in the business landscape through practical experiences, skill development, and exposure to industry leaders.

Motto:

Transcending Leadership.

Our Activities

1. Workshops and Seminars:

Enhance classroom knowledge.

Foster problem-solving skills.

Provide a platform for hands-on learning.

2. Signature Events:

‘Embezzled’, ‘Transcend’, ‘No Limit to 60 Minutes.’

Promote understanding of business complexities.

Facilitate networking with the business community.

3. Skill-Building Events:

Develop students’ knack for solving real-world cases.

Evaluate and prepare students for National-Level Case Competitions.

4. Collaborative Partnerships:

Partners: Unilever, Marico, bKash, British American Tobacco, Nestle, etc.

Offer opportunities for internships, mentorship, and industry exposure.

What We Do

Arrange

Organize

Institutional events i.e. workshops, seminars, industrial tours etc.

Publish

All types of institutional publications i.e. Newsletter, Resume Book etc.

Discuss

Sessions to improve communication & presentation skills
