Crack The Case

Crack The Case is a yearly specialized Business Case Solving Workshop designed for IBA-JU Business students interested in navigating the world of Business Competitions. Our respected alumni along with other renowned teams in the business competition arena, who have excelled in such competitions before, join us to clarify the process of solving business cases.

Learning from the firsthand experiences of seniors serves as a significant motivator for the students. All inquiries about team formation, analysis, presentation, and common challenges are addressed as they generously share their journey to success.

In line with past editions, Crack The Case consistently aims to support newcomers in the realm of Business Competition. This year marked the 5th season of the event, bringing valuable insights and perspectives for the juniors.
